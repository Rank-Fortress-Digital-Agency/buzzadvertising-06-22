What is Digital Marketing?


Digital marketing refers to the process of using online platforms such as websites, social media networks, and email newsletters to promote a company’s products or services. It is a broad term that can include a variety of different strategies such as website design, search engine optimization (SEO), pay-per-click advertising (PPC), and email marketing. Essentially, digital marketing is any strategy that uses electronic channels to reach and engage customers.

Why Use Digital Marketing?


There are many reasons why businesses should invest in digital marketing. First and foremost, online platforms provide an easy way for companies to connect with new customers. In addition, online platforms allow businesses to target specific demographics based on interests and behaviors, which makes it easier to reach the right consumers with relevant ads and content.
